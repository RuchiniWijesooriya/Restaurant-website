About Us

Well come to Foke & Flame

Our restaurant is dedicated to serving freshly prepared meals made with premium ingredients, fire-grilled flavors, and a passion for quality. We believe every meal is more than just food — it’s an experience, carefully crafted to satisfy your taste buds and warm your heart.

With a cozy atmosphere, friendly service, and an ever-evolving menu, Fork & Flame is built on love, tradition, and culinary creativity. Whether you're dining in with family, enjoying a quick lunch, or celebrating a special moment, we aim to make every visit unforgettable.

Our Mission

To serve tasty, hygienic, and affordable food while providing outstanding customer service.

Our Vision

To become a trusted and loved restaurant brand by creating joy and satisfaction for all customers.

Meet Our Chefs

Chef Kasun Perera - Head Chef

Chef Kasun Perera is the heart of our kitchen with over 10 years of culinary experience in Sri Lankan and Western cuisine. He has worked in well-known restaurants and hotels, mastering the art of flavor balance and food presentation

    Specialities

    • Sri Lankan & Western Fusion Cuisine
    • Signature Burgers & Pizzas
    • Rice & Main Dishes
    • Menu Development & Food Presentation

Sous Chef - Chef Nimal Fernando

Chef Nimal Fernando brings 7 years of experience and strong technical skills to the kitchen. He supports the Head Chef in daily operations and ensures consistency in taste and quality.

    Specialities

    • Pasta & Pizza Preparation
    • Kitchen Operations
    • Quality Control
    • Food Safety & Hygiene

Pastry & Dessert Chef - Chef Ruwan Silva

With 5 years of experience, Chef Ruwan Silva specializes in desserts and beverages. His creativity adds the perfect sweet finish to every meal.

    Specialities

    • Desserts & Milkshakes
    • Beverages & Iced Coffee
    • Plating & Presentation
